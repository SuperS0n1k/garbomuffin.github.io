PAY 1 SAT/BYTE

This is the smartest fee choice EVER. You pay almost nothing (talking cents, potentially not even plural) and your transaction usually gets confirmed quickly.

Sites like this one and this one can recommend really high fees but that's all fake news inaccurate.

BEST CASE SENARIO:

Transaction is confirmed in the next block for almost nothing because the mempool is empty.

AVERAGE CASE SENARIO:

Transaction takes a couple hours to confirm.

WORST CASE SENARIO:

Transaction gets dropped from the mempool and you get your coins back. This is incredibly rare and if you use RBF and increase the fee this should never happen.

Use Replace By Fee

If your wallet supports it, enable RBF (replace by fee) to be able to increase the fee later if need be.
This way you can still pay almost nothing in fees under normal conditions and increase the fee if it is absolutedly required or to speed up confirmation time.
