Why pay 1 sat/byte?

Why not? If you don't have to pay a large fee, then don't!

BEST CASE SENARIO:

Transaction is confirmed in the next block for almost nothing because the mempool is empty. (at the time of writing this is normal)

AVERAGE CASE SENARIO:

Transaction takes a couple hours to confirm.

WORST CASE SENARIO:

Transaction gets dropped from the mempool and you get your coins back.

Tips

  • Enable opt-in RBF. This allows you to increase your transaction fee if needed.
  • (once it's finished) Use the lightning network.
  • (once wallets support it) Use segwit

Here's some transactions I made using low fees. The ones that took more than a couple blocks were sent out when the mempool was full or being spammed.

  • 1.027 sat/byte: confirmed after ~8 minutes, ~1 block
  • 1.097 sat/byte: confirmed after ~222 minutes, ~22 blocks
  • 1.265 sat/byte: confirmed after ~32 minutes, ~3 blocks
  • 2.618 sat/byte: confirmed after ~684 minutes, ~68 blocks
  • 2.743 sat/byte: confirmed after ~11 minutes, ~1 block
  • 2.959 sat/byte: confirmed after ~1 minute, ~1 block
