Restricted Mode Bypass (for YouTube)

Get around restricted mode on YouTube!

If it sees that the video you're trying to watch is restricted it will create a "Watch it anyway" button. Click on that to watch it anyway!

Due to the way that this has to work, I really would encourage you to try to minimize the times you have to use it.

It also might violate the YouTube TOS.

There might be a 10 second delay before the video starts to show, everything else should be explained in the UI.
