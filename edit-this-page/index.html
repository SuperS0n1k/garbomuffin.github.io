Edit This Page (v0.1)

A simple bookmark that lets you edit the text of any website!

This is really a dumbed down version of X-Ray Goggles by Mozilla.

Installation

Drag the following link onto your bookmarks bar: Edit This Page Don't click on it, drag it onto your bookmarks bar!

You may have to enable your bookmarks bar, that dependson the browser. It's in your setting somewhere.

Usage

After installation simply click on the bookmark to edit the page.
