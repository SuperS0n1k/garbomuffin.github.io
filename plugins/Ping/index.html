Ping

A very simple plugin that lets you get the ping of your players in milliseconds! (with permissions and configs)

Source

If you wanna look at it or anything.

Permissions

(all are default)

ping

Access to the /ping command itself. Does not in itself provide any functionality.

ping.self

The ability to get one's own ping.

Children:
  • ping

ping.other

The ability to get someone else's ping or their own.

Children:
  • ping
  • ping.self

ping.*

Access to all permissions in this plugin.

Children:
  • ping
  • ping.self
  • ping.other
